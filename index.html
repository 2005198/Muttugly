About Us

Since 2006 Muttugly was groomed over 60,000 dogs! All shapes, sizes, breeds and temperaments. Our groomers are some of the most patient, talented and caring animal lovers in the industry. All either City and Guilds or IPET trained. If you need advice, want to show off your new puppy or learn some grooming tips, pop in for a chat. We might teach you a trick or two!
